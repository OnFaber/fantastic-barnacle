7. Your Rights under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you are granted additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These rights include:

Right of Access:
The right to request copies of your personal data (Article 15 GDPR).
Right to Rectification:
The right to have inaccurate or incomplete data corrected (Article 16 GDPR).
Right to Erasure:
Also known as the "right to be forgotten", the right to have your data deleted (Article 17 GDPR), subject to certain conditions.
Right to Restrict Processing:
The right to request the restriction of processing your data under certain circumstances (Article 18 GDPR).
Right to Data Portability:
The right to obtain and reuse your personal data for your own purposes across different services (Article 20 GDPR).
Right to Object:
The right to object to the processing of your personal data in certain situations (Article 21 GDPR).
Right Not to Be Subject to Automated Decision-Making:
Including profiling, unless explicitly agreed or legally required (Article 22 GDPR).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@website.com. If you are not satisfied with our response or believe that your rights under the GDPR have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
